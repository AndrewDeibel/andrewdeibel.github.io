This means while scaling your browser your gutters remain the same width! All gutters are horizontally and vertically spaced.

Spacing

Easily space components with margin and padding with customizable sizes. Default sizes include: xsmall (2px), small (4px), medium (8px), large (16px), xlarge (32px). You can optionally enable automatic spacing scaling for different screen sizes. This will automatically change the spacing of components by 1/2 on tablets and phones. Additionally spacing can be set for each box side individually (top, right, bottom, left).
